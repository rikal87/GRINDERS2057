## 📌 주요 업데이트 요약 (2026.03.24)

1. AI Logic & Difficulty Rebalancing
Early Game Scaling: Lowered the precision of early-stage personas to make the learning curve smoother.

Strategic Aggression: AI now attempts aggressive bluffs more frequently when they have zero showdown value.

Draw Persistence: Opponents are now more "sticky" or aggressive when holding draws. Expect them to barrel more often or chase with tenacity.

2. New Systems: Drunk & Tilt
Dynamic Gameplay: We’ve noticed that early-game personas could feel a bit too passive, leading to repetitive or "boring" stretches. These new systems are designed to break that monotony and create more unpredictable, high-stakes moments.

Drunk Status: Certain stages now feature "Intoxicated" personas. Expect them to be more talkative and play a much looser, "splashy" style. They’re here to gamble, not just fold.

Tilt System: Losing a big showdown now has a chance to trigger Tilt. Tilted AI will show a significant spike in aggression—watch out for emotional over-betting and reckless bluffs.


3. AI Agent System Overhaul
Tutorial: Added a dedicated guide on how to utilize AI Agents effectively.

Progression: AI model subscriptions are now level-restricted to better match player growth.

Multitasking: You can now assign the same task to multiple agents simultaneously.

New Tasks & UI: Added several fresh tasks and polished the Agent interface for better clarity.

4. Quality of Life (QoL)
Fast Forward: Added a "Fast Forward" feature that skips directly to the next hand after you fold.

Note: This can be toggled On/Off in the Settings menu.

5. Story Expansion
Florence: Her recruitment timing is now earlier. Related story beats have been revised and expanded for better flow.

Mid-game Content: New main story chapters have been added to the mid-game arc.

6. Audio & Music
Implemented a formal tracklist system and refreshed the BGM for a better cyberpunk vibe.
